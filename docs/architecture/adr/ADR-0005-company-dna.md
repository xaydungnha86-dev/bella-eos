# ADR-0005: 4-Tier Company DNA Enterprise Asset

* **Status**: Approved & Frozen
* **Date**: 2026-07-22
* **Author**: Enterprise Architecture Board

## Context
Generic AI agents lack corporate alignment, tone consistency, and risk governance. Hardcoding tone or rules inside individual prompts makes them rigid and non-reusable across different departments.

## Decision
We define **Company DNA** as an independent **Enterprise Asset (v1.0)** organized into 4 distinct tiers:
1. **Identity DNA** — Vision, Mission, Core Values, Corporate Culture.
2. **Brand DNA** — Voice, Tone, Design Principles, Content Rules, Colors.
3. **Business DNA** — SOPs, Policies, Risk Thresholds, Decision Rules.
4. **Operating DNA** — Risk Appetite, Delegation Matrix, Approval Rules, Decision Style, Compliance Level.

## Consequences
- Company DNA can be published, updated, or traded as DNA Packs on **Bella Marketplace**.
- Guarantees 100% alignment across all AI Workers with executive vision and risk policies.
